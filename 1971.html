1971

Drop Out

On September 16 1971 Steve Wozniak dropped out of the University of California.
He left with a degree in electrical engineering.
Fun fact Steve Wozniak met Steve Jobs in his senior year on univeristy.
Steve Wozniak was 21 at the time of his leaving.
